About

The School is located in Saddar, the heart of the city of Karachi, is listed as a heritage building, we teach 
children from Nursery to Matric. Our newest premises, opened in 2020, houses our KG to Matric level students.

The School is entirely independent, receiving no grant from the government or any other source.

The Principal chairs the Senior Leadership Team which is made up of The Principal and The Heads of Sections.

In each section there are Assistant Heads and a Senior Master and Mistress of School Section, who collectively 
make up the Internal Management Team together with the Principal and Heads of Sections.

The Central Administration, deals with security, finance, maintenance, IT, purchasing and special projects.
