ADMISSION NOTICE 2024


Dear Parents and Guardians,
We are pleased to outline the Admission process for 2024. In the Kindergarten Section only Nursery will be open for admission.

Please note that submission of any fake documents will invalidate the application for admission. This is strictly enforced.

STEP 1:
The documents listed below are to be submitted.

Documents required for verification [for all applicants] are:
1. Original birth certificate of the candidate (not a recent one) with photocopy.
2. Original B-form issued by NADRA / Nadra Birth Registration / NICOP, with photocopy of the candidate.
3. Recent passport-size photograph of the candidate (2 copies) with the candidate's name clearly written on the back.
4. Original CNIC/NICOP and photocopy of both parents.
5. If applicable, we request parents to bring profile in addition to the documents above.
6. A recent family photograph of parents with the child and siblings taken in 2024.


STEP 2:
Saturday 20th January 2024: A date and time will be posted against your form number on the website for verification of documents.
Monday 22nd to Saturday 27th January 2024: Verification of documents is to be done in person by parents.
The date for your interview and the child's assessment will be given to you at this time.

STEP 3:
Onsite interview of both parents and the child on the specified date and time.
The child will be assessed on the same day as the parent's interview.

STEP 4:
Results will be posted on the website using the form numbers on Tuesday 30th April 2024.

Any queries regarding admission, please email:
nursery.admissionsn@tjs.edu.pk

Muhammad Junaid
Principal
